Dear Parents

Welcome to the Academic Year 2021_22 - a new session of learning and growth for our students. We extend our gratitude for your faith in us and for the encouragement in our endeavours.

This session we are geared up in preparing our students with hybrid learning, enhanced co-curricular activities and life skills. We have upskilled ourselves with an EdTech platform that will allow teachers and students:

  • To write on the whiteboard
  • To share their thoughts with much more engagement
  • To have quicker shorter assessments to gauge learning while teaching
  • To share screen options
  • To share notes that can be saved
  • To know the status of student engagement
  • To use break-out rooms
  • To share external links
  • To use poll options
  • To have longer duration of live classes – synchronous
  • To use enhanced assessment tools

The USP of the EdTech platform is that it helps us have an integrated approach to defining the life cycle of a student - prior to admission, the admission process, the performance of the student, co-curricular tracking, parental portal which allows access to track student work through a 360-degree lens, interact with teachers and much more.
Use it and Explore it.

Global Citizenship is a new concept that will be conducted as part of the CCA. You will learn more during the orientation of the Principal.

NCFE CONNECT is a new helpline that we will be stretching out to parents with the school so that we can solve queries and provide solutions.

Practice to perfection is a tool that has already been implemented to help reiterate concepts. You will find further enhancement in this regard.

Thank you for the encouragement and support extended thus far and we look forward to a year of overcoming challenges together with trust and faith in each other.

Warm regards
